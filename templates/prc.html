PRC Curve

The Precision-Recall Curve (PRC) visually represents a classification model's performance, particularly in imbalanced class distribution scenarios. It illustrates the trade-off between precision and recall for different classification thresholds. Precision measures the model's accuracy in predicting positive instances, while recall (sensitivity) quantifies its ability to identify all positive instances correctly. The PRC curve is generated by varying the classification threshold and plotting precision-recall pairs. The Area Under the Curve (AUC-PRC) summarizes the overall performance, the PRC curve helps evaluate and compare models, especially in imbalanced class scenarios.
